## Getting help

1. Search **existing [issues](https://github.com/tageecc/circle/issues)**.
2. Open a **bug report** or **feature request** using the repository [issue templates](https://github.com/tageecc/circle/issues/new/choose).
3. For **security-sensitive** matters, read **[SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md)** — do not file public issues.

## Community standards

Interactions are governed by the **[Code of Conduct](CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md)**.

## Commercial support

Circle as a product may offer separate support channels via [the project homepage](https://circle.com). This repository’s issue tracker is for **open-source collaboration**, not guaranteed SLA support.
